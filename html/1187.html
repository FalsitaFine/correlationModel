Sources for Stroke Guidelines and Consensus Statements

  • The Cochrane Database of Systematic Reviews (CDSR) – Stroke Group
    Abstracts of > 40 systematic, updated reviews of various aspects of stroke therapy. Full reviews are available by personal or institutional subscription. Search Medline for Cochrane stroke review abstracts.
  • American Academy of Neurology – Guideline Statements
    Large listing of guidelines, advisories and parameters.
  • US National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NIH) – Cardiovascular Information for Health Professionals
    Current guidelines and interactive web sites for primary prevention treatment of hypertension, obesity and cholesterol.
  • National Guideline Clearinghouse
    Sponsored by the US Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality
  • American College of Cardiology
    Practice guidelines and quality standards — sorted by topic.
  • Mobile Medica – Apprisor
    Downloadable guidelines for Palm and other handheld devices. Includes a full selection of stroke management and prevention recommendations.
